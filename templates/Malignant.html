Malignant Tumors

Treatment: Treatment options include surgery, chemotherapy, radiation therapy, targeted therapy, immunotherapy, or hormone therapy.

Duration: Treatment duration varies widely depending on tumor type and stage, ranging from weeks to years.

Recovery Time: Recovery time varies from weeks to months, with ongoing monitoring and follow-up care.

Malignant Brain Tumors

Treatment: Treatment involves surgery, radiation therapy, chemotherapy, targeted therapy, or a combination of these.

Duration: Treatment typically spans several months to a year or more.

Recovery Time: Recovery can be challenging and may involve ongoing rehabilitation, with recovery times varying widely.

Malignant Melanoma

Treatment: Treatment includes surgical excision, immunotherapy, targeted therapy, chemotherapy, or radiation therapy.

Duration: Treatment duration varies based on cancer stage and chosen therapies, ranging from weeks to several months.

Recovery Time: Physical recovery may take several weeks, with ongoing surveillance for recurrence.

Malignant Lymphomas

Treatment: Treatment involves chemotherapy, immunotherapy, radiation therapy, or stem cell transplantation.

Duration: Treatment duration varies depending on subtype and response to therapy, ranging from several months to longer durations.

Recovery Time: Recovery may involve immune suppression and increased infection risk, with recovery times varying.
